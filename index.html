Life in Shanghai

I grew up and live in Shanghai, a city with high population density and active economy. Shanghai is modern, international, and fast-paced.

Living in Shanghai for more than 20 years, I have got used to the fast-paced life here. I love Shanghai because this is a place where people can seek for a lot of opportunies and unlock their potentials.

Life in SJTU

I received my bachelor's degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). We have a very big campus. There are four libraries and more than ten canteens. Yet, each library is full of people every day, and the canteens are always crowded during lunchtime and suppertime. Although life in SJTU is busy, it is also enjoyable.

Study Abroad

In 2019, I took the Winter Program initiated by UM-SJTU Joint Institute and went to Technische Universität Berlin for a one-month study.

We had a great time there. At the end of our study, we spent a few days in Switzerland and had a great time skiing in Mürren.
